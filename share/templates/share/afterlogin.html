About Web App

This web application is a type of online content hosting service where users can store plain text,images,articles etc. and conveniently share with others with just one link. Moreover, it also stores the generated links each time you share something so that you can refer them again. This was built on the top of django framework.This was done under the supervision of Prof Biju Ram Mohan for IT290 Seminar Course.
